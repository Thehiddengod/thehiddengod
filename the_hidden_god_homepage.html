The Hidden God

“Every breath they took was borrowed from me.”

The Hidden God is an epic anime-inspired series rooted in ancient Egyptian mythology. It follows Anum, a forgotten prince of the gods, as he rediscovers his divine power and battles rising forces of chaos threatening to unravel the realms. Alongside his fierce allies—Noxira and Kosey—Anum faces monsters, betrayals, and revelations that could change the fate of gods and mortals alike.

This is more than a battle of strength. It’s a war of identity, memory, and divine legacy.
